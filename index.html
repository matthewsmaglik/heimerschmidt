About

HEIMER SCHMIDT

Development + Production


Heimer Schmidt is an production company with a passion for developing bold, cutting edge stories that leave a lasting impression. Our projects blend creativity, intelligence, and authenticity—crafting cinematic experiences that are as meaningful as they are memorable. The company is led by a team of award-winning producers and writers with a keen eye for storytelling that resonates. Inspired by innovators and boundary-pushers, the teams background merges years of creative expertise with an unwavering commitment to delivering thoughtful, character-driven narratives. Whether through genre-bending features or smart, unconventional tales, Heimer Schmidt thrives on telling stories that challenge, excite, and endure.
